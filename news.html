Auditions Open: A Midsummer Night's Dream — Fall 2025

We are now accepting registrations for our Fall 2025 production, Shakespeare's beloved romantic comedy A Midsummer Night's Dream. All experience levels are welcome. Auditions will be held on June 7 & 8 at the Dryden Performing Arts Centre. No prepared monologue required — all you need is a willingness to play.

Awards

Theatre 17 Wins Four Awards at NW Ontario Theatre Festival

We are proud to announce that our Fall 2024 production of Steel Magnolias was honoured with four awards at the Northwestern Ontario Theatre Festival, including Best Production, Best Director (Margaret Holloway), Best Lead Actress (Claire Ashworth), and Best Set Design (Brian Tachuk). Congratulations to the entire team.

Community

Annual Fundraiser Gala — June 14, 2025

Our Annual Fundraiser Gala returns on Saturday, June 14th at the Dryden Golf & Curling Club. An evening of dinner, live entertainment, a silent auction, and fellowship. Proceeds support our youth theatre program and the bursary fund. Tickets are $65 per person and available through this website or at the door. Tables of eight available.

Spring Season Announced: The Importance of Being Earnest

Theatre 17 is thrilled to announce our Spring 2025 production: Oscar Wilde's sparkling masterpiece, The Importance of Being Earnest. Directed by Margaret Holloway, this production will run from April 25 to May 4 at the Dryden Performing Arts Centre. Tickets go on sale April 1.

Youth Theatre Workshops — Registration Open

Theatre 17's popular Youth Theatre Workshops return in January. Sessions run every Saturday morning from January 11 through March 29 and are open to participants ages 10–17. No experience required. Registration is $80 for the full series. Space is limited — contact us to register.

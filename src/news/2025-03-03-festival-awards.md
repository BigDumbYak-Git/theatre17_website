---
title: Theatre 17 Wins Four Awards at NW Ontario Theatre Festival
date: 2025-03-03
category: awards
summary: Our production of Steel Magnolias took home Best Production, Best Director, and two acting honours.
---

We are proud to announce that our Fall 2024 production of *Steel Magnolias* was honoured with four awards at the Northwestern Ontario Theatre Festival:

- **Best Production**
- **Best Director** — Margaret Holloway
- **Best Lead Actress** — Claire Ashworth
- **Best Set Design** — Brian Tachuk

Congratulations to the entire cast and crew. This is a testament to the extraordinary talent and dedication of everyone involved in the production.

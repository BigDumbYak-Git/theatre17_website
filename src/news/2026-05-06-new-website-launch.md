We've got exciting news that has nothing to do with stage lights or curtain calls — theatre17.ca is officially live, and we are so happy to welcome you to our brand new home on the web!

After quite a bit of behind-the-scenes work (we know a thing or two about those), the new site is up, polished, and ready for you to explore. Here's a quick tour of what you'll find:

**What's On** — The homepage always shows you what we're currently up to, whether we're deep in rehearsals, calling for auditions, or announcing our next production. Dates, venue info, and ticket links are all right there front and centre.

**News & Announcements** — You're reading it! This is where we'll post audition calls, show announcements, awards, and anything else worth sharing. Bookmark this page or check back often — we've got a busy season ahead.

**Show Archive** — Head over to the archive to browse our production history. You can filter by year, see show posters, and click into any production for full details. This is a work in progress — we've been producing shows since 2006 and we're working our way through the history — but the goal is to have every single show we've ever done documented right here. Cast lists, crew credits, the works. If you remember a show we've missed or have details we haven't captured yet, please reach out and let us know!

**Members & Bios** — Meet the people who make Theatre 17 run. The Members page features bios for current members and alumni, and you can filter by performers, crew and creative, or browse everyone at once. Same as the archive, we're still adding people from our history, so if you don't see yourself yet — you will!

**Get Involved** — Thinking about joining us? The Contact page is the place to start. Whether you want to be on stage, help build the set, run the sound board, or just find out what we're up to, we'd love to hear from you.

We're really proud of how this site has come together, and we're even more excited about what it will be once we've finished filling in all the history. Theatre 17 has had some incredible shows and some incredibly talented people involved over the years, and they all deserve to be celebrated here.

Thanks for stopping by — see you at the theatre!

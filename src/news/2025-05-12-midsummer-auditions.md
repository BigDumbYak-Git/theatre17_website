We are now accepting registrations for our Fall 2025 production, Shakespeare's beloved romantic comedy *A Midsummer Night's Dream*. Directed by Margaret Holloway, auditions will be held on **June 7 & 8** at the Dryden Performing Arts Centre.

No prepared monologue required — all you need is a willingness to play. Cold readings will be provided on the day.

**We also need crew!** If you're interested in set construction, costumes, lighting, sound, or stage management — note this on your registration form. No audition required for crew positions.

Register using the Auditions form on this site or contact us directly to arrange a time.

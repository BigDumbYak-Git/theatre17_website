Our People

Members & Bios

The cast, crew, and creative community that make Theatre 17 possible.

DF
Current Member

Daniel Forsythe

Actor

Daniel has been performing with Theatre 17 since 2018. His recent roles include Jack Worthing in The Importance of Being Earnest and Tom in Steel Magnolias.

Shows: Earnest • Steel Magnolias • Orient Express • Charlie Brown
CL
Current Member

Claire Ashworth

Actor

Claire joined Theatre 17 in 2020 and quickly became one of the group's most versatile performers. She has appeared in seven productions and also assists with workshop facilitation.

Shows: Earnest • Steel Magnolias • Wizard of Oz • Clue
MH
Current Member

Margaret Holloway

Director & Actor

Margaret has been directing with Theatre 17 since 2015. A veteran of over twenty productions, she brings a meticulous eye for period detail and character development to every project she takes on.

Director: Earnest • Steel Magnolias • Orient Express • Sound of Music
PS
Current Member

Patricia St. Laurent

Actor

Patricia is one of Theatre 17's most celebrated performers. Her portrayal of Lady Bracknell earned rave reviews. She has been a member since the group's early years and has appeared in over thirty productions.

Shows: Earnest • Boeing Boeing • Orient Express • Wizard of Oz (and many more)
AT
Current Member

Andrea Tsai

Stage Manager

Andrea is the organizational backbone of every Theatre 17 production. As Stage Manager, she coordinates all technical rehearsals, cues, and production schedules with extraordinary calm and precision.

Stage Manager: Earnest • Steel Magnolias • Orient Express • Wizard of Oz
BT
Current Member

Brian Tachuk

Set Design & Construction

Brian's set designs have transformed the Dryden Performing Arts Centre into Victorian drawing rooms, train compartments, and yellow brick roads. A carpenter and designer with an extraordinary creative eye.

Design: Earnest • Steel Magnolias • Charlie Brown • Sound of Music
RK
Current Member

Ryan Kowalczyk

Actor

Ryan brought tremendous energy and comic timing to the role of Algernon in this season's production of The Importance of Being Earnest. He has been with Theatre 17 since 2022.

Shows: Earnest • Steel Magnolias • Boeing Boeing
WN
Alumni

Walter Nieminen

Actor & Former Artistic Director

Walter served as Artistic Director of Theatre 17 from 1998–2014 and appeared in over forty productions. His dedication helped establish the group's reputation for high-quality community theatre.

Retired 2014 after 26 years with Theatre 17
LM
Current Member

Lena Morrison

Costume Design

Lena's costumes are legendary in the region. Her award-winning Victorian wardrobe for The Importance of Being Earnest took six months to construct. She has been costuming for Theatre 17 since 2010.

Costumes: Earnest • Sound of Music • Orient Express • Wizard of Oz
